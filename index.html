About Us

We are CTRL+SHIFT+CODE, a team of passionate Grade 10 students from Birla Public School, Qatar who specialize in coding and love creating immersive experiences using Alice 3.

Our journey began with a shared passion for coding and 3D animation, which led us to form a team that consistently pushes the boundaries of what's possible with Alice 3.
